# Getting Started

## Installation

### From Source (Developer Mode)

1. **Clone the repository**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/zied-vvd/BehindTheSite.git
   ```

2. **Open Chrome Extensions**
   - Navigate to `chrome://extensions/`
   - Enable "Developer mode" (toggle in top right)

3. **Load the extension**
   - Click "Load unpacked"
   - Select the `BehindTheSite` folder

4. **Complete onboarding**
   - The onboarding page opens automatically on first install
   - Select your concerns and tags
   - Start browsing!

## First Steps

### 1. Complete the Onboarding

When you first install, you'll be guided through a 3-step setup:

1. **Select your concerns** - What matters to you? (Environment, Privacy, Labor Rights, etc.)
2. **Choose tags to track** - Based on your concerns, select specific flags to watch for
3. **Confirm and start** - Review your choices and begin browsing

### 2. Browse Normally

Visit any website. If we have data on the company and it matches your selected tags, a banner appears at the top showing:

- Company name
- Country of origin
- Major shareholders
- Relevant warning/info flags

## Troubleshooting

### Banner not showing?

1. **Check if the site is in our database** - We're community-driven and don't have every site yet
2. **Check your tag preferences** - The banner only shows if the site matches your selected tags
3. **Try refreshing** - Click üîÑ in the popup to fetch fresh data

### Extension not working?

1. Go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Find BehindTheSite
3. Click the refresh icon or toggle it off and on
4. Check for errors in the console (click "Errors" if shown)
